Frequently Asked Questions

Protein Explorer is a web application that allows users to search for proteins, analyze protein sequences, and explore protein structures. The application provides a user-friendly interface to access protein data from various databases and perform sequence and structural analysis.

You can search for a protein by entering the UniProt ID, gene name, or protein name in the search bar on the Protein Explorer homepage. The application will retrieve the protein information and display it in the search results. You can then click on the protein to view detailed information and perform further analysis.
